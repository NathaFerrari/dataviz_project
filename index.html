Well-Being evolution during crisis

This is the project subtitle

Sasha Bravo, Nathan Ferrari, Andrea Frati, Manuel Grosso

Introduction to the European context

Over the past two decades, Europe has experienced a series of economic and geopolitical shocks that have had a direct impact on citizens' well-being: the 2008 financial crisis, the sovereign debt crisis, the COVID-19 pandemic and, more recently, the conflict in Ukraine. To visualise how European countries have responded to these events, we have constructed an Aggregate Wellbeing Index that combines economic, social and stability dimensions.

Geographical distribution of well-being

The interactive map shows how the level of well-being has evolved over time in different European countries. The darker colour indicates a higher index value, while the lighter colours represent lower values.

Qui inseriremo l'analisi dei pattern geografici principali: es. Nord Europa con valori più alti, Est Europa più basso, recuperi progressivi nel Sud, ecc.
